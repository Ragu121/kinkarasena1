JPS Mission

JPS Mission

Some of Srila Prabhupada's Instruction to Srila Gurudeva

1. Get a rose and offer it to Srila Prabhupada every day to increase his longevity.
2. Have ISKCON's own place in Kolkata before Srila Prabhupada's arrival.
3. Allowed me to grow own food in Mayapur.
4. Make a road to the Ganges so he could take bath every day in the Ganges.
5. Chant 16 rounds and follow the four regulative principles.
6."I give you the spiritual world and you develop it." To develop Mayapur into the spiritual capital of the world.
7. Make it (Mayapur) a city where 25,000 to 50,000 people can live.
8. Build the Temple of Vedic Planetarium.
9. Accommodate brahmacaris and grehastas in Mayapur.
10. Think big.
11. While living in Kolkata temple Srila Prabhupada said that if he leaves his body in Vrindavana then his sarira samadhi should be built in Vrindavana and puspa samadhi in Mayapur and vice versa.
12. Guru Maharaja was made the lifetime Chairman of Bhakthivedanta Swami Charity Trust.
