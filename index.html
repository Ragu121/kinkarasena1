Join Our Team


We have a huge pool of service opportunities in various domains such as Preaching, Engineering, Architecture, Information Technology, Project Management and Administration.

1. Open minded and willingness to learn new dimensions
2. Ability to work independently
3. Attentive to details
4. Strong organization and management skills
5. Writing speaking and editing skills in English
6. Either full time (6-8 hours a day) or part time (6-8 hours a week)
